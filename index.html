Welcome to the MIDAS.LAB!

We are an interdisciplinary research group of scientists and physicians
from the Department of Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology
of the University Hospital in Tuebingen.

Research

Our aim is to advance methods and applications for processing and analysis of medical imaging data using machine learning methods.

Specifically, we are working on the analysis of multiparametric PET/MRI and PET/CT data aiming for functional characterization of tissues and tumors in vivo. Our second focus is the development and implementation of methods for image quality assessment, image enhancement and automated image analysis in epidemiological MRI studies.

Projects

  • Machine Learning Methods

    Development and implementation of machine learning models for Medical Image Processing.

  • Clinical Applications

    Research projects in oncological and epidemiological imaging.

  • Teaching

    Workshops on quantitative data analysis for life scientists.

  • Infrastructure

    Hardware and software infrastructure to support clinical research.
